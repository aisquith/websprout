🌱 Gardening tips

    
Get lush results
every time

    
Better prompts grow better sites. These habits make the biggest difference.

    

      
1
Be ruthlessly specific
Don't say "nice design." Say "minimalist, off-white #f7f5f0, Playfair Display headings, 80px section padding." Specificity removes guesswork.

      
2
Name your stack
Say "vanilla HTML/CSS/JS only" or "no frameworks." Without this, the AI may choose a stack you can't maintain.

      
3
Preview before you pay
Re-generate as many times as you like for free. Only click Unlock when you're truly happy with what you see in the preview.

      
4
Reference real sites
"Inspired by Stripe's homepage" or "similar vibe to Linear" gives AI a concrete target and drastically improves output.

      
5
Ask for mobile-first
Always include "fully responsive, tested at 375px." AI defaults to desktop — don't let your site wilt on small screens.

      
6
Weed before deploying
After downloading, scan for Lorem Ipsum, "yourdomain.com" placeholders, and missing alt tags before going live.
